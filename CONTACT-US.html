To be a Power Dental USA official distributor
Power Dental USA, Inc. is proud to take requests from future distributors of our fine line of dental instrumentation, from anywhere in the world. Should you have any questions or need further information, or to request a distributor application and agreement to complete, please contact us by emailing your requests and questions to info@mydentalusa.com.



We will require references for you and your company, as well as credit references from other businesses you deal with. We understand that newly started businesses may not have many references to check, so if that is the case with your company, personal references will be accepted along with personal bank and credit references. Power Dental USA, Inc. prides itself on helping to keep the economy moving. We will accept applications for distributorships and check all references prior to sending out any wholesale pricing. Should we need further verification from anyone wishing to become a distributor, we will contact you.
