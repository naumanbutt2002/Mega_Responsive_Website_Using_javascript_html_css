Customer Support
Frequently Asked Questions
1. What is the turnaround time for orders?
Answer : Usually within a week.

2. Do we retip cavitrons?
Answer : We do not but we will replace your used instrument for a new one with a very low repair fee.

3. Do we retip orthodontic cutters etc?
Answer : We do not but we will replace your used instrument for a new one with a very low repair fee.

4. Do we ship out of the country?
Answer : Yes we do.

5. How do you know when an in instrument cannot be sharpened again?
Answer : Our trained instrument professionals inspect each and every instrument that comes to us for sharpening and retipping. We believe it is better to be safe than sorry where sharpening is concerned. We will not sharpen a blade that is too short or too thin as this may injure the patient or doctor during a procedure should it break off. Safety is our main concern.

6. Do we make the instruments made by other manufacturers?
Answer : Yes we can cross reference any instrument from other manufacturers for you.

7. Can you get an estimate before work is done?
Answer : Yes. We are happy to provide an estimate.

8. Can we change the tips that are on an instrument to make it a different instrument?
Answer : Yes we can on most instruments. It all depends on the size of the opening on the handles.

9. Can we make an instrument especially for you?
Answer : Yes we can but depending on the instrument, the price may be slightly higher and specialty instruments are not returnable as they are made for a specific person.
10. What happens if an instrument cannot be sharpened or retipped?
Answer : We will offer you the option of replacing the instrument at a lower cost as we will take your old instrument as a trade-in.

11. Notice : Website concerning substitution of items:
Dental USA reserves the right to substitute items at any time, based on availability.

12. Rewards/Cash back?

Answer : 5% of any online purchase will be applied to subsequent orders.
With our new rewards program, you can earn 5% of your total instrument purchase, as cash back (minus shipping costs), toward your next purchase of any new instrument when ordering only from our website. You must have an online account to participate. Cash back not eligible on phone, email or faxed orders or service orders. Please add your Account : www.mydentalusa.com

Please feel free to call us with any other questions you may have. The above questions represent just a few of our usual questions from customer.
